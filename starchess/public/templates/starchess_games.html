StarChess

StarChess is a chess variant on a star-shaped board, and very easy to pick up if you know chess. Original rules here.

Starcraft Chess

Starcraft Chess is way different than chess, and has more of a resource creation / RTS feel. Original rules here.
Note that I've changed the rules slightly - rooks can now move 1 non-taking square diagonally as well as their normal up and down.

Note: Invite a friend to join any new game by sending them the link to the game page; they will have to log in first.
There is also a clearly-marked option avaiable on the new game page to play a rudimentary AI.
